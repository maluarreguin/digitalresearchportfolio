Digital Curation

Capstone Project
Created for Digital Humanities 299: Digital Curation
Winter 2023

Link to Project

For my Capstone Project, I created my own digital exhibition using Mozilla Hubs. This project encompassed the entirety of Winter Quarter and allowed me to develop my skillsets with 3-D modeling softwares, curatorship, design, metadata, and archival materials through the utilization of the UCLA Digital Library Collections. My digital exhibition was titled, "Angeleno in Images: An LA Legacy of the Mexican American Perspective", and was sourced almost entirely from the Los Angeles Latino Families Photo Project. This exhibition aimed to illustrate the lives of Mexican American Angelinos - their preservation of self, expansion of identities, and ardor for exultation over the twentieth century. Through examining the themes of family, devotion, and celebration, the exhibition hoped to preserve the lives of these individuals for the enrichment of Los Angeles cultural history.
